# Why Now Is a Critical Moment for DeAI
## 1. What is DeAI?
Definition
Decentralized AI (DeAI) is a deep integration of blockchain technology, distributed computing, and artificial intelligence, which reconstructs the entire process of AI training, inference, data, model, computing power, and governance  in a decentralized manner.
What problem does it aim to solve? 

### The Three Dilemmas of Centralized Artificial Intelligence: 
**Data Monopoly and Opacity**
- Tech giants such as OpenAI and Google control the training process of data and models 
- Developers are unable to verify the results of Model Training and inference
Make AI outputs traceable, verifiable, and shareable. 

**Incentive Imbalance and Sovereignty Deficit**
- User data is collected and used without restrictions
- Training data, model fine-tuning, and Agent contributors receive no benefits


**Enable contributors to share value, Computing power monopoly and high costs**
- GPU resources are controlled by a few cloud providers and large enterprises
- API pricing power lies in the hands of the platform (prices may increase or services may be suspended at any time)
- Small and medium-sized developers find it difficult to afford the training costs
Turn idle GPUs into a global AI public computing power pool.

## 2. Why choose DeAI now?
### Historical Background: The Convergence of Three Forces
 - Major premise: AI has entered the era of infrastructure
Since 2022, ChatGPT has triggered a wave of AI application enthusiasm; by 2025, AI will evolve from a "tool" to a "core system", and every application will require AI capabilities;
But at the same time, everyone has seen the pain points of centralized platforms, such as cost, censorship, and stability 

 - Macro Environment: Regulation and Geopolitics Drive Decentralization
The European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) has strengthened the supervision of data privacy; the Sino-US technological competition has accelerated the demand for Localization deployment; developers are seeking AI infrastructure that is not "choked"... 

 - Industry Development: Web3 Seeks Real Application Scenarios
After the DeFi and NFT booms, the blockchain industry needs new narratives, and DeAI has emerged as the most convincing implementation direction for "blockchain + AI" ÔºåBy 2025, DeAI may drive the Web3 supercycle, similar to the DeFi Summer of 2020

### Industry Signals: Dual Influx of Capital and Developers
- Investment Boom: The influx of VCs such as a16z and Polychain, focusing on distributed computing and AI infrastructure, with over $100 million in financing in the DeAI track (Bittensor, Ritual, Gensyn, etc.); according to DefiLlama data, in the first eight months of this year, cryptocurrency projects focused on artificial intelligence raised a total of $516 million. Juan Leon, a senior investment strategist at Bitwise, predicts that by 2030, this "mega trend" could add $20 trillion to global GDP.
- Developer growth : Open source development reached record levels this year, with contributions to public code repositories reaching 1.12 billion times (up 13% year-over-year); 6 of the 10 fastest-growing code repositories are AI infrastructure projects, and it is expected that by 2025, nearly 20% of popular projects among users participating in open source projects for the first time will focus on the field of artificial intelligence.
